About

I am a recently graduated PhD researcher working at the intersection of constitutional law, urban governance, and human rights. My doctoral thesis — The Constitutional Role of Municipalities in the Governance of Social Rights — compared how Turin, Barcelona, and Athens translate the rights to education, health, and housing into administrative practice, under the supervision of Prof. Giuseppe Martinico.

I work fluidly across Spanish, Italian, French and English, with reading knowledge of German — and I am currently directing that range toward European policy and international‑organisation roles.

Data project ↗ El vacío del alquiler social — Spain in the European context An interactive policy brief comparing Spain's social housing stock with the EU and across autonomous communities, extending the doctoral thesis's core question to open public data.
